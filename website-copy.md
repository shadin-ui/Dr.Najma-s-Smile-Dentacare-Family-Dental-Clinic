## H1: Frequently Asked Questions

### 1) Will dental treatment be painful?
Most treatments are much more comfortable than people expect today. We use a gentle approach and proper pain-control methods to reduce discomfort. If you are anxious, tell us first — we will guide you calmly.

### 2) I am scared of root canal treatment. Is it very painful?
This is a very common fear. In reality, modern root canal treatment is usually done with good pain control and is often less painful than living with an infected tooth. We explain each step so you feel more confident.

### 3) How much will treatment cost?
Costs depend on your dental condition and treatment choice. After examination, we provide clear guidance on expected charges and options so you can decide comfortably. Typical costs may vary within **[FEES RANGE]** depending on treatment.

### 4) What happens during the first visit?
Your first visit usually includes discussion of symptoms, a dental check, and a clear treatment plan. We answer your questions and explain next steps in simple language. You can then decide the timing that suits you.

### 5) How long does an appointment usually take?
A routine consultation often takes around 20–40 minutes, depending on your concern. Some procedures may require longer time or multiple visits. We’ll let you know the expected duration before we begin.

### 6) Do you treat children?
Yes, we welcome children and focus on making them feel comfortable. We use a calm, friendly style so visits feel safe and positive. Parents are guided at every step.

### 7) Can anxious patients get extra support?
Absolutely. Many patients come to us with fear, and we take that seriously. We go slowly, explain clearly, and proceed only when you feel ready.

### 8) Do you follow proper sterilisation and hygiene?
Yes. Patient safety is a top priority in our clinic. We follow strict cleaning and sterilisation protocols for instruments and treatment areas.

### 9) Do I need an appointment, or are walk-ins allowed?
Appointments are recommended to reduce waiting and ensure dedicated time. Walk-ins may be accommodated based on availability. For faster support, call or WhatsApp before coming.

### 10) Can I visit if I have sudden tooth pain?
Yes, if you are in pain, contact us immediately. We do our best to provide same-day assessment whenever possible. Early treatment can prevent bigger problems.

### 11) Do you explain treatment options before starting?
Always. We believe you should understand what is being done and why. You will receive clear explanations and options before treatment begins.

### 12) How often should I do a dental checkup?
For most people, once every 6 months is ideal. If you have gum issues, frequent cavities, or ongoing treatment, we may advise more frequent visits. Regular checkups help avoid emergency pain.

### 13) My gums bleed while brushing. Is that serious?
Bleeding gums are common but should not be ignored. It may indicate early gum inflammation that can be treated effectively. Book a checkup early for easier recovery.

### 14) Can you help with smile improvement?
Yes, we provide basic cosmetic options like whitening and simple smile enhancement. We focus on natural-looking results that suit your face and comfort level. A consultation helps choose the best approach.

### 15) How can I book an appointment quickly?
You can call **[PHONE NUMBER]** or message us on WhatsApp for quick booking support. Share your concern and preferred time, and our team will assist you.
